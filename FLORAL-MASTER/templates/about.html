About Fresh Loaves

Fresh Loaves is dedicated to the artisan craft of sourdough breads and fine pastries. Our passion for quality and tradition drives us to create baked goods that are not only delicious but also made with the best ingredients.

Our Philosophy

Fresh Loaves brings you the finest sourdough breads, baked with love and care. The best local flours, our sourdough culture, and a good amount of patience and love give our loaves the distinct flavors of naturally leavened breads, each one shaped by hand just for you.

Our Story

Fresh Loaves was started by Dina Weber, an artisan baker with a deep passion for the craft of sourdough baking. Located in Mysore, Karnataka, our bakery is a place where tradition meets innovation, and every loaf of bread is crafted with the finest ingredients and utmost care.

Our Values

At Fresh Loaves, we believe in quality, authenticity, and sustainability. We are committed to using locally sourced ingredients, supporting small-scale farmers, and reducing our environmental footprint. Every product we make reflects our dedication to the craft of baking and our love for food made with care and integrity.
